Core Pillars

Engineered for clarity.

Eight architectural decisions that make Minty unlike anything you've used before.

01
The Bento UI System
Inspired by modular Japanese design, our Bento-style dashboard organises your financial life into clean, interactive blocks — frosted glassmorphism, spring physics, and tactile haptic feedback. Credits pulse UP, Debits press DOWN. UI that actually feels alive in your hands.
Spatial Discovery · Spring Physics
02
24-Hour Micro-Insights
A fixed timeline chart centred on "Now" — so you always understand where you stand in the present moment, not just at month end.
Temporal Context
03
SMS Intelligence Engine
Reads your bank SMS alerts natively on-device. You swipe your card → Minty logs it. No cloud servers, no 3rd party SDKs, zero manual entry. Powered by a hyper-optimised native Kotlin engine.
Zero-Effort · On-Device
04
Multi-Wallet Architecture
Personal and Savings wallets free. Unlock Pro for 10 wallets. Elite for unlimited. Global base-currency reconciliation instantly.
Smart Budgeting
05
AI Financial Advisor
Not a chatbot — a ruthless, personalised analyst. It knows your income, spots your leaks, and hits you with hard truths: "You spent ₹4,200 on Zomato this week… that's 38% above normal."
Personalised Analysis
06
Haptic Feedback Engine
Heavy, medium, and light haptic layers woven into every transaction — so each financial decision feels significant and reinforces discipline.
Tactile Control
07
Shake-to-Add Mode
For cash transactions. Physically shake your phone on the home screen and the expense adder blasts open in <0.3s. The fastest manual input ever designed for a finance app.
0.3s Launch
08
Layered Architecture
Riverpod reactive state, Hive NoSQL, atomic JSON services, and isolate-based Workmanager background tasks — smooth 120fps UI, secure local vault.
Flutter · Riverpod · Hive · Kotlin

Privacy First

Paranoia-Level Protection.

Financial data is your most sensitive data. Minty is built with paranoia. You are the user — not the product.

Zero-Knowledge SMS
The SMS engine runs 100% locally. No API call, no cloud transfer. Bank messages never leave your device.
ON-DEVICE ONLY
Local-First Database
Your transaction data stays on your device. Period. Hive NoSQL with AES-256 encryption at rest.
LOCAL STORAGE
Ghost Mode Sync
Only profile metrics are cloud-backed. Actual transaction amounts are firewalled in encrypted local storage — never synced raw.
ENCRYPTED
No Trackers. Ever.
No ad-caching, no data selling, no 3rd party analytics SDKs inside the app. TLS 1.3 in transit. Servers hosted in India.
ZERO ADS
