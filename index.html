Core Pillars

Engineered for clarity.

Six architectural decisions that make Minty unlike anything you've used before.

01
🍱
The Bento UI System
Inspired by modular Japanese design, our Bento-style dashboard organises your financial life into clean, interactive blocks — curated to reduce cognitive load and deliver instant spatial awareness. Real-time summaries of "This Week" and "This Month" positions, all at a glance.
Spatial Discovery
02
📊
24-Hour Micro-Insights
A fixed timeline chart centred on the "Now" — so you always understand where you stand in the present moment, not just at month end.
Temporal Context
03
💰
Multi-Wallet Architecture
Personal and Savings wallets free. Unlock Super Wallets via Razorpay for specialised budgeting. Global base-currency reconciliation across all wallets, instantly.
Razorpay Powered
04
📳
Haptic Feedback Engine
Heavy, medium, and light haptic layers woven into every transaction — so each financial decision feels significant and reinforces discipline.
Tactile Control
05
📨
SMS Intelligence
Automated parsing of bank alert SMSes for zero-effort entry. Permission-based, privacy-first. Your transactions, captured before you blink.
Zero-Effort Entry
06
🏛️
Layered Feature-Driven Architecture
Riverpod reactive state, Hive NoSQL, atomic JSON services, and isolate-based Workmanager background tasks — so the UI stays smooth while the vault stays safe.
Flutter · Riverpod · Hive
